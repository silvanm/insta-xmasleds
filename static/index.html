Instagram driven Christmas lights

It's Christmas time and every homeowner decorates his house blinking lights... more

I am also one of them. But I wanted to add a pinch of IoT and scoop of art to my installation. It pulls images with the hashtag #christmas from Instagram and uses them to light up the RGB-LEDs which are attached to our terrace. This visualization is a live view of the image which is currently shown.
